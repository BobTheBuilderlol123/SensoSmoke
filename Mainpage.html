Vaping & Mental Health Awareness

The truth about vaping, its risks, and innovative ways to keep schools safe

Understanding Vaping & Mental Health

Vaping has been promoted as a safer alternative to smoking, but the reality reveals serious concerns. The chemicals in vape products may disrupt brain chemistry, increasing anxiety, depression, and other mental health issues — especially in teenagers, whose brains are still developing.

Research shows a growing correlation between vaping and symptoms of mood disorders. Protecting mental wellbeing means informing students, parents, and educators about these hidden risks.

Preventive education and early intervention play a vital role in reducing the vaping epidemic's impact on young minds.

Introducing the VapeGuard Stall Detector

SmokeGuard is a smart, compact smoke detection device designed for individual stalls in schools. Once it detects smoke or vapor, it automatically triggers a camera to capture a photo of the student inside, helping school officials take prompt, responsible action.

The captured photo is instantly sent securely via a specialized app directly to the principal for review — creating a safer and healthier environment.
