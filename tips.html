Tips to Help You Quit Vaping

Every journey begins with a first step. Here are practical ways to stay committed and overcome cravings.

Daily Actions

  • Set a quit date and make a clear plan
  • Replace your vape with gum, mints, or a stress ball
  • Remove all vape products from your home, car, and backpack

Craving Management

  • Drink water — staying hydrated helps reduce urges
  • Use distraction — go for a walk, listen to music, or text a friend
  • Remind yourself why you’re quitting — better health, more energy, and freedom

Track Progress

  • Celebrate smoke-free milestones
  • Keep a journal of what helps and what doesn’t
  • Share your wins with the support group
