Protect Our Students from Vaping

Educating schools and communities about the dangers of vaping and mental health impact on youth.

What is Vaping and Why Itâ€™s Harmful

Vaping is the act of inhaling vapor produced by electronic cigarettes or similar devices. Many students are unaware that vaping delivers harmful chemicals and nicotine, which can harm lung health and cause addiction. Besides physical harms, vaping disrupts brain development that continues until young adulthood.

Impact on Mental and Physical Health

Studies show adolescents who vape have elevated risks of anxiety, depression, and issues with attention and memory. Nicotine addiction can exacerbate stress and interfere with learning at school. Social isolation may rise as peer pressure to vape increases. Our mission is to empower students with knowledge and support to stay healthy and vape-free.

Impact Over Time

First time vaping impact: Nicotine triggers dizziness, coughing, and throat irritation. The brain experiences a hit of nicotine increasing dopamine, starting addiction pathways.

SmokeSense: Vape Detection for Schools

SmokeSense is an innovative device designed specifically to detect smoke or vaping inside school bathroom stalls. Once it detects smoke, it automatically takes a photo and sends it securely to the principal, helping enforce a vape-free zone to keep students safe.
