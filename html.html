Health Impact Education

Understanding the connection between vaping, physical health, and mental wellbeing.

Physical Health Effects

Respiratory Damage

E-cigarette aerosol contains harmful chemicals that can cause lung inflammation and permanent damage to airways.

Brain Development Impact

Nicotine exposure during adolescence can harm the developing brain, affecting memory, concentration, and impulse control.

Cardiovascular Risks

Vaping increases heart rate and blood pressure, potentially leading to increased risk of heart disease.

Mental Health Connection

Increased Anxiety

Nicotine dependence can exacerbate anxiety symptoms, creating a harmful cycle of use to manage symptoms.

Depression Risk

Studies show teens who vape are more likely to develop depression and experience mood disorders.

Social Isolation

Addiction can lead to behaviors that damage relationships and create social disconnection.

VapeSense Detection System

Our comprehensive solution helps schools detect vaping, support student health, and create safer environments.

Advanced Detection Technology

  • Chemical Sensing

    Specialized sensors detect aerosol particles and chemical compounds unique to vaping products.

  • Privacy Compliant

    No cameras or microphones - our system only detects vaping-specific chemical signatures.

  • Real-Time Alerts

    Immediate notifications sent to administrators when vaping is detected.

  • Easy Installation

    Discrete, tamper-resistant design that integrates with existing infrastructure.

How VapeSense Works

A streamlined system for detection, notification, and support.

1

Detection

Sensors installed in bathrooms and discreet areas detect chemical signatures unique to vaping.

2

Notification

System immediately sends alerts to designated staff members via mobile app and dashboard.

3

Response
